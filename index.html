About Me

With zero professional experience but unlimited curiosity, I'm embarking on an exciting journey into the world of marketing. My passion lies in understanding how brands connect with people, how psychology shapes consumer decisions, and how creativity drives growth.

Why MBA Marketing?

Marketing is the intersection of art and science. It's about telling compelling stories while leveraging data. It's about understanding human behavior and creating meaningful connections between brands and consumers. That's the challenge that excites me.

My Marketing Mindset: I believe in questioning everything—why consumers behave the way they do, what makes a brand memorable, how trends emerge, and how to craft strategies that resonate with diverse audiences. I'm here to learn, innovate, and grow.
